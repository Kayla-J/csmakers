Introduction to Design Thinking
Lesson description: The purpose of this lesson is to introduce Design Thinking through a hands-on activity where each two people will design name tags for each other.
Time required: 45 minutes
Alabama DLCS standards: 8th grade:
  • Computational thinker - Abstraction #2
  • Innovative designer - Design thinking #29
