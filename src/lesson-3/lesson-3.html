Introduction to Computer Science and the micro:bit
Lesson description: This lesson will prepare students with a quick overview of basic computer science terminology and concepts then introduce the BBC micro:bit microcontroller hardware and Javascript Blocks Editor software tool.
Time required: 55 minutes
Alabama DLCS standards: 8th grade:
