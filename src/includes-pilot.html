8th Grade Lessons & Materials

Welcome to the CS Makers lessons and materials site. This site is a repository of 8th grade computer science lessons and resources from the NSF INCLUDES CS Makers grant. The CS Makers curriculum is aligned with the Alabama course of study on Digital Literacy and Computer Science. Each lesson set includes computer science materials for teachers and students with making activities that incorporate the BBC micro:bit.
